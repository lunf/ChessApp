# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Chesswise is designed as a local chess app. We do not sell personal information, track users across apps or websites, or include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## Information We Collect

Chesswise stores the following information locally on your device:

- Current chess game state
- Move history
- App settings, such as mentor preferences and response language settings

This information is used only to restore your game, configure the chess experience, and provide app features.

## AI Mentor

Chesswise includes an optional AI mentor feature. When you ask the mentor for guidance, the app may use Apple Foundation Models where available on your device.

The mentor request may include:

- The current board position in FEN format
- The move history
- The current Stockfish best move, when available
- Your mentor settings and prompt preferences

This information is used to generate the mentor response. Chesswise does not operate its own server for mentor requests.

## Stockfish

Chesswise includes Stockfish as an on-device chess engine. Stockfish analyzes chess positions locally on your device. Chesswise does not send Stockfish analysis data to our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Chesswise may rely on Apple system services, such as Apple Foundation Models, when available. Use of Apple system services is subject to Apple's privacy practices and device settings.

## Data Storage

Game state and settings are stored locally on your device using app storage. Deleting the app removes locally stored app data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

Chesswise is not designed to collect personal information from children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be included with the app or made available where the app is distributed.
